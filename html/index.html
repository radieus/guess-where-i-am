FAQ

What is this?

This is a game that uses Geoguessr as inspiration.

How does this work?

This site uses Leaflet open-source JavaScript library to help you make your guess about your localisation and Google Street View API to help you explore the world.

But aren't Google APIs paid?

They are indeed, therefore to get around this pricing strategy I require you to upload your own API key. This way Google knows who they are billing - in this case you are billed.

But I want to play for free!1!!1!

Don't worry, Goggle offers 300$ free credit, which will be more than sufficient your gaming nights.

How do I get an API key?

Go here and register to get an API key.

Once you have a key save it against your profile here.

Is this safe?

Your keys won't be used for anything other than their stated purpose: namely, for each individual's games on this site. Apart from your email, no personal details are stored on our side.
